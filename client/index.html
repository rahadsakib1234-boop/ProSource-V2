The AI Assistant is powered by Google's Gemma model, running as a Supabase Edge Function. It reads your actual live CRM data — invoices, leads, clients — and gives contextual financial and lead analysis. Your data is never stored or used to train any model.
Stripe charges in USD ($99/year). Your bank converts to BDT at their current rate — approximately ৳11,000/year. Visa and Mastercard issued in Bangladesh work on Stripe. bKash integration is on our roadmap.
You can export two versions of your data. The private export includes everything — supplier names, supplier links, cost prices, and margins. The client-safe export strips all sensitive data, leaving only what's appropriate to share with buyers. This protects your sourcing relationships and margins.
Completely. ProSource uses Supabase Row-Level Security with strict multi-tenant isolation enforced at the database level. Every client, product, invoice, and file is invisible to any other account — this isn't just application-level filtering.
Company accounts support unlimited employees, each with module-level permissions you control. Personal accounts are single-user by design.
You keep full access until the end of your billing year. After that, your account is locked but all data is retained for 30 days so you can export everything before it's removed.
